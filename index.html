Course Curriculum

Your Course Outline

A carefully sequenced curriculum that builds your skills from foundation to fashion. Every lesson is designed to deliver real confidence and results.

01
Master Basic Crochet Stitches

Chain, slip stitch, single, half double, double and treble crochet. These are the foundational stitches every crocheter must master first.

02
Shaping Techniques

Learn how to shape your crochet fabric with intention. Understand row shaping, working in rounds and building structure into your pieces from the very start.

03
Color Changing Art

Unlock the magic of multi color designs. From clean color carries to bold color blocks, your work will become visually striking and expressive.

04
How to Increase, Decrease and Weave in as a Pro

Three essential skills combined in one power module. Shape your work with precision, control your stitch count and finish every piece with clean invisible thread ends.

05
Stitch Variations
Mesh Stitch
Open, airy and elegant. Perfect for tops and bags with a high fashion feel.
Granny Square Patterns
The timeless classic reimagined for modern makers. Join motifs into stunning garments and accessories.
Shell Stitches
Fan shaped repeats that add texture, rhythm and luxury to any project.
06
Bralette
Top
Dress
Creation of Wearable Projects such as Bralettes, Skirts and Shorts

The showstopper module. You will design and construct real wearable crochet fashion pieces you can wear, gift or sell with full confidence.

🏆
Capstone · Final Module

Your Real-World Graded Project

Every student completes a final project evaluated by our instructors a true test of everything you've learned. This is a portfolio piece that proves your skill and marks your growth from beginner to confident crocheter. Your grade is your badge of honour.
